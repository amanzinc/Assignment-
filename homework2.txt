2 Objective
Build a complete Structure from Motion (SfM) pipeline from video sequences to:
ΓÇóEstablish feature correspondences across frames.
ΓÇóEstimate the Essential Matrix and recover relative camera motion via epipolar

ΓÇóPerform Bundle Adjustment to refine poses and 3D structure.
ΓÇóBuild a consistent sparse 3D map and localize unseen frames against it.
